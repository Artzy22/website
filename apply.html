FAQ

Common questions, straight answers

What does the revenue partnership actually mean?

We come in as a partner, not a vendor. We bring our proven offer, our marketing engine, and our setter team. You provide the chairs, the practitioners, and a $2K monthly ad budget paid directly to Meta. We share a percentage of the revenue we generate together. No retainer. If we don't deliver, you don't pay. Our average partner LTV is around $160K, with our top two clinics generating $500K each.

How are pre paid deposits handled?

Patients put down a $189 or $299 deposit before their appointment is confirmed. That money goes straight to your clinic. It's the single biggest reason our no show rate is near zero. Patients with skin in the game show up.

What's the 100% ROI guarantee?

If, in 90 days, we haven't generated more in patient revenue than you've invested in ad spend, we keep working for free until we do. Our incentives have to be aligned with yours.

How fast will I see results?

Most clinics start seeing pre paid bookings within 10 to 14 days of go live. The first $5K to $15K in tracked patient revenue typically lands within 30 days.

Why only one clinic per local area?

Because we run paid traffic in your zip codes. If we worked with two clinics in the same market we'd be bidding against ourselves and driving up your costs. We won't do it, even if it means turning down clients.

What if I'm under $10K per month?

Apply once you cross that threshold. The partnership model and the $2K ad spend only make sense at that level. Below it, you're better off focusing on organic content and referrals first.
