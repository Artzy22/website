Frequently asked

Honest answers to the questions every clinic owner asks before applying.

Who do you partner with?

Established med spas and aesthetic clinics generating $10K and above per month in revenue, offering high end treatments such as lasers, RF microneedling, body contouring, and injectables. We accept four new partners per quarter.

How does the revenue partnership work?

We come in as a partner, not a vendor. We share a percentage of the revenue we generate together rather than charge you a retainer. You bring the chairs and the practitioners. We bring the offer, the marketing engine, and the setter team. Average partner LTV with us is around $160K, and our top two clinics have generated $500K each. Most partners maintain a $2K monthly ad budget paid directly to Meta, not to us. That's the fuel for the system.

How quickly will I see results?

Most partners begin receiving qualified consultation bookings within 10 to 14 days of go live. Cash collected typically follows within 30. We've had partners book consultations on day three.

What is included in the partnership?

The full stack. Our proven high margin offer (battle tested with patients paying a $189 to $299 deposit and a $4K average ticket on the upsell), paid media on Meta, weekly creative production, our setting team for every call (virtual or in person), payment processing, automated onboarding (Slack, Drive, AI call summaries), and a dedicated partnership manager who integrates into your click to close process.

Do I need to do anything?

Show up to consultations. Provide a treatment. Approve creative concepts. That's the whole list. Our team handles everything else end to end.

Why is the application required?

Because we partner with a small number of clinics at a time. The application ensures fit before either of us spends 45 minutes on a strategy call. It saves both of us time.

What if I'm under $10K per month?

You're welcome to apply once you cross that threshold. The revenue partnership and ad spend only make sense at that level. Below it you're better served by organic content and referrals.

Can I see real partner results?

Of course. Visit our testimonials page for case studies, video interviews, and revenue screenshots from actual partners.

Is there a contract?

A 90 day initial term so we have time to optimize. After that month to month. We don't believe in trapping clinics into long agreements. If we're not delivering, you should be free to leave.

What if I no show the strategy call?

You won't be able to reschedule. We hold one slot per applicant and respect everyone's time. Add it to your calendar the moment you book.

How is Newly Booked different from other agencies?

Most agencies sell ads and walk away. We come in as a growth partner with a proven high margin offer, a setting team (virtual or in person) that handles every call, and we integrate into your click to close process. We get paid when you do, not before. That alignment is why our average partner LTV is around $160K.
